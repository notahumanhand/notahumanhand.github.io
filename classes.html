Mage

If you chose Mage as your class at first level, you gain the following benefit:
You gain 2 levels of proficiency in K-Focus.

Primary Class Bonus

If you chose Acolyte as your class at first level, you gain the following benefit:
You gain 2 levels of proficiency in C-Will.

Arcane Expertise

You may add your Knowledge score to your Arcana pool. When casting spells, use your Knowledge in place of your Charisma to determine Spell Bonus and damage. In addition, you gain a +2 aptitude bonus to K-Magic checks.

Favoured School

Choose one school of magic, which you must start with at least one spell from. When casting spells from that spell list, reduce the Arcana cost by 1, to a minimum of 1.

Spells

Mages gain access to the following spell lists: Red, Orange, Gold, Green, Blue, Purple, Brown.
Mages start with 6 spells, and gain 2 spells per level up.

Sneak Attack

You know where to strike to make your attacks hurt. When you make an attack with a weapon with the Finesse or Ranged property, and you have an ally within 5 feet of the target or have advantage on your roll against the target, you may add an additional 1d8 damage on a hit. This damage increases by an additional 1d8 at levels 5, 10, 15, and 20.

Finesse Weapons Training
