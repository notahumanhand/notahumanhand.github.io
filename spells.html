Arcane Quill

Cantrip

Implement

Spellbook

Casting

Basic Action

Duration

Indefinite

Range

N/A

Effect

You manifest a spectral quill that you can use to write with. Anything you write with this quill can only be understood by creatures or groups you designate at the time of writing. In addition, at any point, you can remove all traces of any or all writing with this quill. Any writing done with this quill counts as a magical effect for the purposes of detection and dispelling.

Short

Arcana Cost

Concentration

No

Effect

As a reaction when a creature within range falls from a height high enough to deal damage, you may slow their descent. The target of this spell falls at a rate of half their speed per round downwards, and does not take damage when reaching the ground. Once the target reaches solid ground, the spell ends.

Raise Zombie

Death Magic

Type

Creation

Casting

Basic Action

Duration

Encounter

Range

Short

Arcana Cost

7

Concentration

No

Effect

Choose a corpse within range. That corpse becomes animate, using the Zombie stat block, for the duration, rolling its own initiative and taking its own turn in combat. It follows verbal commands that you issue to it. You can make a Swift Action each turn to command the zombie. If you do not issue any commands, the zombie will make the Dodge combat manoeuvre until it runs out of Prowess.
