Alchemy

Rank 1

Class

Mage

Prerequisites

None

Incompatibilities

None

You have the ability to turn spells into potions. Spend one hour and the chosen spell's Arcana cost to create a single-use potion. This potion can be consumed as a swift action, with the spell’s effect centred on or targeting the creature consuming the potion. In addition, potions can be thrown at a target to affect them with the spell instead. To throw a potion, the attacker may make a spell attack with the alchemist’s spell bonus against a creature within Short range, contested by the target’s Physical Defence. On a hit, the target is affected by the spell stored in the potion. On a miss, the potion is lost and the spell does not take effect.

Arcane Amplification

Rank 1

Class

Mage

Prerequisites

None

Incompatibilities

None

Whenever you cast a spell, you may spend additional Arcana to increase the amount of damage you deal. If the spell’s damage is d4s or d6s, spend 1 Arcana to increase the number of damage dice by 1. If the spell’s damage is d8s or d10s, spend 2 Arcana to increase the number of damage dice by 1. If the spell’s damage is d12s, spend 3 Arcana to increase the number of damage dice by 1.
