Human

Humans were Lillon's creation, the deity of balance. They are one of the most widespread species in DulÅ«n, as well as the most common. They are highly adaptable to any habitat, with generalised skills that can be applied to nearly any situation. Humans are size Medium.

Primary Trait: Astounding Resilience

Humans get a +1 to their level in any one of the four saving throws.

Secondary Trait: Adaptable

Humans get a +1 to any skill level of their choice.

Tertiary Trait: Multicultural

Humans get a +1 to their K-World skill level. In addition, when determining points for language, they get two additional points.
